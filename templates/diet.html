Healthy & Balanced Meal Plan

For people with diabetes, it's important to have a well-balanced and controlled diet to help manage blood sugar levels. Food should be balanced, containing a combination of protein, healthy fats, and fiber-rich carbohydrates to prevent rapid spikes in blood sugar. Here's a suggested menu for diabetes patients from Sunday to Saturday.

Meals Sunday Monday Tuesday Wednesday Thursday Friday Saturday
Breakfast
  • Scrambled eggs with spinach and tomatoes
  • Whole-grain toast or a small portion of sweet potato
  • A small bowl of mixed berries
  • Greek yogurt with a sprinkle of cinnamon
  • Chopped walnuts or almonds
  • Sliced apple or pear
  • Oatmeal or unsweetened almond milk
  • Topped with sliced bananas and a few crushed almonds or chia seeds
  • Avocado toast on whole-grain bread
  • Poached or boiled egg on top
  • Sliced cucumber or a side of cherry tomatoes
  • Smoothie: Spinach, kale, or mixed greens blended with unsweetened almond milk
  • A handful of frozen berries and half a banana for natural sweetness
  • Optional: Add a scoop of protein powder (without added sugars)
  • Low-sodium smoked salmon or turkey slices
  • Whole-grain crackers or rice cakes
  • Sliced bell peppers and carrots on the side
  • Cottage cheese or tofu with a drizzle of olive oil and a sprinkle of black pepper
  • Whole-grain toast or a small serving of quinoa
  • Fresh fruit salad (mix of berries, melon, and kiwi)
Mid-morning snack
  • 1 small apple or pear
  • 1 ounce (about 28g) of unsalted mixed nuts (almonds, walnuts, and pistachios)
  • Carrot sticks or cucumber slices
  • Hummus for dipping
  • 1 hard-boiled egg
  • A small bunch of grapes
  • 1 small orange

  • Cherry tomatoes or baby carrots on the side
  • 1 small banana
  • 1 ounce (about 28g) of cheese (such as cheddar or mozzarella)
  • 1/4 cup of mixed unsalted nuts and seeds (almonds, pumpkin seeds, sunflower seeds)
  • 1/4 cup of fresh blueberries or raspberries
Lunch
  • Grilled chicken breast or tofu
  • Quinoa salad with mixed vegetables (e.g., bell peppers, cucumbers, cherry tomatoes)
  • Light vinaigrette dressing (olive oil, lemon juice, and herbs)
  • Mixed greens salad with sliced hard-boiled eggs
  • Avocado slices and cherry tomatoes
  • Grilled shrimp or salmon
  • Dressing made with olive oil and balsamic vinegar
  • Turkey or vegetable wrap with a whole-grain tortilla
  • Hummus or mashed avocado spread
  • Lettuce, cucumber, and bell pepper slices as fillings
  • Lentil or black bean soup
  • A side of roasted vegetables (e.g., broccoli, cauliflower, and carrots)
  • Grilled or baked fish (e.g., cod, tilapia) or a vegetarian alternative
  • Steamed asparagus or green beans

  • Sliced strawberries or mandarin oranges

  • Stir-fried vegetables (e.g., broccoli, bell peppers, snap peas) with tofu or lean beef

Snacks
  • Greek yogurt (unsweetened) with a sprinkle of cinnamon
  • Handful of mixed berries (e.g., blueberries, raspberries)
  • Celery sticks or cucumber slices
  • 2 tablespoons of almond butter or peanut butter (without added sugars)
  • Rice cakes or whole-grain crackers
  • Guacamole or salsa for dipping
  • 1 small orange or clementine
  • 10-12 raw almonds or walnuts
  • Baby carrots or cherry tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons of hummus or tzatziki
  • Cottage cheese (low-fat) or Greek yogurt (unsweetened)
  • Sliced strawberries or peaches
  • Hard-boiled egg
  • 1 small apple or pear
Dinner
  • Baked chicken breast or tofu
  • Roasted sweet potatoes
  • Steamed broccoli or green beans
  • Grilled fish (e.g., salmon, trout) or a vegetarian alternative
  • Quinoa salad with mixed vegetables
  • Light vinaigrette dressing (olive oil, lemon juice, and herbs)
  • Stir-fried tofu or lean beef with a variety of non-starchy vegetables (e.g., bell peppers, zucchini, mushrooms)
  • Cauliflower rice or brown rice as the base
  • Baked or grilled turkey meatballs
  • Zucchini noodles (zoodles) with marinara sauce (without added sugars)
  • Stuffed bell peppers with a mixture of lean ground turkey, quinoa, diced tomatoes, and spices

  • Baked or grilled chicken or fish

  • Vegetable curry with chickpeas or lentils
